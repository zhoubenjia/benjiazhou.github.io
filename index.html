Biography

Artificial Intelligence · Computer Vision · Multimodal Learning

Benjia Zhou received the Ph.D. degree in Artificial Intelligence and is currently an Assistant Professor with the Beijing Institute of Technology, Zhuhai. Previously, he was an AIGC Research Scientist with Tencent GY-Lab. He has published more than 15 papers in leading journals and conferences, including IEEE TPAMI, IEEE TIP, IEEE TCSVT, CVPR, ICCV, AAAI, and ACM Multimedia.

Research Interests
